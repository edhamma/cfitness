Glossary¶

  • concentration: The ability to sustain attention on an object (or objects) with minimal distraction.

  • ñana: (Pali) Insight knowledge.

  • objectify: Turn toward. Look at. See clearly. Take as object. Identify.

  • samadhi: (Pali) The condition or state of concentration, i.e., sustaining attention on an object (or objects) with minimal distraction.

  • samatha: (Pali) Activity of concentration, i.e., sustaining attention on an object (or objects) with minimal distraction.

  • yogi: Person who meditates.
